1. Are the pills vegan?

Yes the pills are vegan, if it doesn't have a vegan trademark on it, the best thing is to contact us or email our company: you'll receive the most up-to-date information from our team.

2. Do you accepts refunds on Heroes, Pills, or Villains?

You may return or exchange the Super Pills purchased from us within 15 days of purchase as long as they are not more than 25% empty. Super Heroes and Villains may be returned or exchanged within 5 days of the purchase date. If under any circumstances the customer is unable to return in that specific time period, we recommend contacting us via email (superpowers@unitedstates.com).

3. Do I have to train my Hero or Villain?

Training your superhero or villain will not be necessary unless the customer desires. Our Heroes and Villains are trained prior to customer purchase. If for any reason you are having issues, please contact superpowers@unitedstates.com; our team would be more than happy to assist.

4. What is the life-expectancy of the Heroes and Villains?

The heroes and villains don't have a exact life expectancy, it is mostly based on how well the customer takes care of them. The average life expectancy for them would be approximately 1-2 years.

5. When will my product be delivered?

During checkout based on your location and the product you have chosen it will provide you an estimated devivery date. There will be options for expidited services as well if needed.
